Create an Engaging Course

It's important to make sure your course is engaging for your students with a well-structured, practical, and rewarding learning experience. The most successful instructors spend time planning their course content before they start recording to ensure their course helps students achieve clear goals.

Course Setup

Step 1: Choose your course topic.

Pick something you're knowledgeable in and genuinely excited about teaching. Learn more about Udemy's Marketplace Insights tool that helps you understand the demand and competition for your course idea.

  • Choose a topic that aligns with your expertise and passion.
  • Research trending topics and analyze the competition using the Marketplace Insights tool.
  • Ensure the topic is relevant and has a potential target audience.
  • Narrow down the subject to something specific and actionable.

Step 2: Define your intended learners and your course's learning objectives.

Before you begin creating content for your course, it's important to define who you are teaching and what your students will be able to accomplish by the end of your course.

  • Identify your target learners and their skill level (beginner, intermediate, or advanced).
  • Write clear, measurable learning objectives for the course.
  • Focus on the skills or knowledge learners will acquire upon completion.
  • Consider how this course aligns with your audience's career or personal goals.
